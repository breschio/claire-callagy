About

Claire Callagy is a photographer based in Martha's Vineyard, specializing in landscape and lifestyle photography. With over a decade of experience capturing moments and places, Claire's work has been featured in numerous publications and galleries across the United States.

Her landscape photography focuses on capturing the subtle interplay of light and nature, revealing the quiet beauty in both grand vistas and intimate natural details. In her lifestyle work, Claire brings the same thoughtful eye to capturing authentic moments and genuine connections.

Claire's clients include leading magazines, brands, and private collectors. She is available for commissioned work and fine art prints.
